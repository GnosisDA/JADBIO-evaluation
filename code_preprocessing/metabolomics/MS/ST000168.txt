PR:PROJECT_TITLE                 	Effect of Insulin Sensitizer Therapy on Amino Acids and Their Metabolites
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	Aims: Prior studies have reported that elevated concentrations of several
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	amino acids (AA), particularly branched chain (BCAA) and aromatic AA predict
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	onset of type 2 diabetes. We sought to test the hypothesis that circulating
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	aromatic AA and related AA metabolites decline in response to the use of
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	sensitizing agents in overweight/obese adults with impaired fasting glucose or
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	diabetes. Methods: We performed a secondary analysis of a randomized,
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	placebo, controlled study conducted in twenty five overweight/obese (BMI ~ 30
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	adults with impaired fasting glucose or untreated diabetes. Participants were
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	to three months of pioglitazone (45 mg per day) plus metformin (1000 mg twice
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	day, N = 12 participants) or placebo (N = 13). We measured insulin sensitivity
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	the euglycemic-hyperinsulinemic clamp and fasting concentrations of AA and AA
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	using ultra-pressure liquid chromatography tandem mass spectrometry before and
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	the three-month intervention. Results: Insulin sensitizer therapy that
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	enhanced insulin sensitivity reduced 9 out of 33 AA and AA metabolites measured
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	to placebo treatment. Moreover, insulin sensitizer therapy significantly
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	three functionally clustered AA and metabolite pairs: i)
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	ii) citrulline/arginine, and iii) lysine/?-aminoadipic acid. Conclusions:
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	in plasma concentrations of several AA and AA metabolites in response to three
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	of insulin sensitizer therapy support the concept that reduced insulin
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	alters AA and AA metabolites.
