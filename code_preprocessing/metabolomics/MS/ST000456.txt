ST:STUDY_TITLE                   	Metabolomic changes during active immunization with anti-METH vaccines.
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	A targeted metabolomics analysis was performed on serum samples from rats
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	immunized with a methamphetamine-like hapten covalently bound to keyhole limpet
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	hemocyanin (KLH) monomers, and controls. The subjects were approximately 300 g
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	adult male Sprague Dawley rats. RTI International RCMRC received pre-immune
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	serum from RI 11-03A (n=12) which is a matched control serum that was collected
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	before starting immunizations, and week 17 bleeds from RI 11-03A (n=12), RI
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	11-03B (n=8), and RI 11-03C (n=12). The A-C nomenclature denotes A) complete
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	antigen with adjuvant, B) complete antigen without adjuvant and C) only KLH
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	carrier protein and adjuvant without a METH-like hapten. The rats’ serum
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	samples were extracted and prepared for a combined flow injection (FIA) and
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	LC-MS/MS assay (Biocrates AbsoluteIDQ® p180 kit). The Biocrates AbsoluteIDQ®
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	p180 kit (Biocrates Life Sciences AG, Innsbruck, Austria) can quantitatively
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	measure ~188 biologically relevant metabolites from five analyte groups
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	(acylcarnitines, amino acids, biogenic amines, glycerophospho- and
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	sphingolipids, and hexoses) on a triple quadrupole mass spectrometer.

SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	combining 10 µL from each phenotypic sample into a 2 mL LoBind eppendorf tube:
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	1. Pre-immune serum from RI 11-03A (n=12) which is a matched control serum that
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	was collected before starting immunizations. The final volume of this phenotypic
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	pool was 120 µL which was vortexed for 30 seconds. Two phenotypic pools
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	(0wk-03A_Pool1 and 0wk-03A_Pool2) of 25 µL each were created. 2. Week 17 bleeds
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	from RI 11-03A (n=12). The final volume of this phenotypic pool was 120 µL
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	which was vortexed for 30 seconds. Two phenotypic pools (17wk-03A_Pool1 and
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	17wk-03A_Pool1) of 25 µL each were created. 3. RI 11-03B (n=8).The final volume
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	of this phenotypic pool was 80 µL which was vortexed for 30 seconds. Two
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	phenotypic pools (17wk-03B_Pool1 and 17wk-03B_Pool2) of 25 µL each were
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	created. 4. and RI 11-03C (n=12). The final volume of this phenotypic pool was
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	120 µL which was vortexed for 30 seconds. Two phenotypic pools
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	(17wk-03C_Pool1and 17wk-03C_Pool2) of 25 µL each were created. Whole Study
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	Pooled QC Whole study pooled QC samples were prepared by combining 25 µL of
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	each phenotypic pool sample created above into a 2 mL LoBind eppendorf tube.
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	This QC pooled sample was then vortexed for 30 sec. Then, three whole study
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	pooled QC samples of 25 µL each were aliquoted into 2 mL LoBind eppendorf
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	tubes. Biocrates Plate Preparation: All samples (Phenotypic pools, whole study
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	pooled QC and study samples) were vortexed for 10 minutes at 5,000 rpm. Samples
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	were then centrifuged at 16,000 rcf for 4 minutes and at 4 °C. A Biocrates p180
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	kit was prepared following the AbsoluteIDQ™ p180 Kit metabolomics procedure.
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	Briefly, an internal standard mix was added to 95 of the 96 wells. Next, zero
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	samples, QC standards and calibration standards were added to their
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	corresponding wells. The study samples, phenotypic and whole study pooled QC
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	Samples (20 µL) were then added to the appropriate wells and dried for 30
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	minutes under nitrogen flow. The plate was derivatized using a 5%
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	phenylisothiocyanate (PITC) solution in (1:1:1) ethanol:pyridine:water (v/v/v)
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	and, then, incubated for 20 minutes at room temperature followed by a drying
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	step under nitrogen flow. An extraction solvent (5 mM ammonium acetate in
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	methanol) was added to all wells. The plate was then shaken and centrifuged.
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	After centrifugation, 150 µL was removed and transferred to a second 96-well
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	plate (LCMS plate). This second plate was diluted with 150 µL of HPLC grade
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	water for a subsequent LCMS (MRM analysis) for measuring amino acids and
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	biogenic amines. All wells in the original plate were diluted with 400 µL of
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	flow injection analysis (FIA) Running Solvent for a FIA-MS (MRM analysis) for
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	measuring lipids, acylcarnitines, and hexose.
