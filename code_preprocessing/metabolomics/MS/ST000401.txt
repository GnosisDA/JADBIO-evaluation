PR:PROJECT_TITLE                 	Inhibition of diamine oxidase promotes uptake of putrescine from rat small
PR:PROJECT_TITLE                 	intestine
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	Metformin, a biguanide molecule, which is used as first line therapy for type 2
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	diabetes. In this study, we would like to investigate the inhibition of an
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	enzyme called diamine oxidase (DAO) (also known as ABP1), by metformin. Based on
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	our preliminary in vitro study using diamine oxidase enzyme, we saw increased
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	level of putrescine with increasing metformin concentrations (see reference
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	PMID: 26335661). This proposed in vivo study was to determine whether metformin
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	could increase putrescine levels and other metabolites in mice. Aminoguanidine,
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	a known inhibitor of DAO, in this study as positive control, following similar
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	study design described in this paper (PMID: 8912017).

#COLLECTION
CO:COLLECTION_SUMMARY            	After 1 hour of treatment, blood was collected from the portal vein. Blood from
CO:COLLECTION_SUMMARY            	the portal vein was cannulated with a fine syringe and blood was aspirated.
CO:COLLECTION_SUMMARY            	Blood was spun down and plasma was collected and stored in -80. Urine was
CO:COLLECTION_SUMMARY            	collected from bladder after 1 hour. Tissues (liver, kidney, intestine) were
CO:COLLECTION_SUMMARY            	collected at the end of 1 hour. Only duodenum and ileum were used for
CO:COLLECTION_SUMMARY            	metabolomics analysis. This study was conducted under the auspices of approved
CO:COLLECTION_SUMMARY            	IACUC procedures in MuriGenics, Inc (Vallejo, CA).

#TREATMENT
TR:TREATMENT_SUMMARY             	Twenty (20) adult rats (male, weighing approximately 200g) were randomized by
TR:TREATMENT_SUMMARY             	body weights and assigned to five treatment groups (saline, aminoguanidine
TR:TREATMENT_SUMMARY             	(positive control), 3 different oral doses of metformin). Before treatment, rats
TR:TREATMENT_SUMMARY             	were starved for 24 hours. On treatment day, saline or aminoguanidine or
TR:TREATMENT_SUMMARY             	metformin will be given to the rat.
TR:ANIMAL_FASTING                	24 hours
#SAMPLEPREP
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	1. Weigh 4 mg tissue sample ( muscle 20 mg) in to a 2.0 ml eppendorf tube. Add
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	1.0 mL extraction solvent to the tissue sample and using GenoGrinder homogenize
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	samples for 45 seconds ensuring that sample resembles a powder. 2. Centrifuge
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	the samples at 2500 rpm. for 5 minutes. Aliquot 2 X 500µl supernatant, one for
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	analysis and one for a backup sample. Store backup aliquot in the -20°C
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	freezer. 3. Evaporate one 500µl aliquot of the sample in the Labconco Centrivap
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	cold trap concentrator to complete dryness 4. The dried aliquot is then
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	re-suspended with 500l 50% acetonitrile (degassed as given) (only for liver
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	and brain samples). 5. Centrifuge for 2 min at 14000 rcf using the centrifuge
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	Eppendorf 5415. 6. Remove supernatant to a new Eppendorff tube. 7. Evaporate the
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	supernatant to dryness in the the Labconco Centrivap cold trap concentrator. 8.
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	Submit to derivatization.
