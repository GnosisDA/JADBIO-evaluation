#PROJECT
PR:PROJECT_TITLE                 	Metabolite changes associated with weight loss
PR:PROJECT_TYPE                  	Feeding study
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	This study aims to identify changes in metabolites that occur in metabolic
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	including liver, adipose (epididymal), hypothalamus, and muscle, as well as
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	in the obese state that are long-lasting and not reversed by weight loss. Mice
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	divided into three groups termed LFD (low fat diet), HFD (high fat diet), and
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	(switch diet from HFD to LFD)(n = 6 per group). All mice were fed normal chow
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	12 weeks of age. The LFD group were fed a low fat diet (10% fat) ad libitum for
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	weeks. The HFD diet group were fed high fat diet (60% fat) ad libitum for 18
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	The SW group were fed high fat diet for 9 weeks, then low fat diet for 9 weeks
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	which their body weights returned to similar levels as the LFD group. Each
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	was analyzed for metabolite and gene expression changes.
