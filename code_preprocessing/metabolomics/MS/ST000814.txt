ST:STUDY_TYPE                    	MS analysis
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	The goal is to look at measures of overall metabolic state in relationship to
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	hormonal environment and measures of mood/depression or cognitive function
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	(behavioral and functional MRI images). There are some relationships in this
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	data set between glucose/insulin levels and cognitive function, and we would
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	like to use the untargeted assays to further investigate this relationship.
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	Factors related to visual/spatial memory are included in the design. Factors 1
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	and 2 (Left Hippocampus, Right Hippocampus) are levels of activation in the
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	hippocampus during a visual memory task, (data missing for some of the samples).
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	Factors 3-5 (BVMT % Retained, BVMT Learning T, BVMT Delay T) are results from a
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	cognitive test of spatial memory function.
