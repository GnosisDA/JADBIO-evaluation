PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	Johanna Lampe (Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center at Univ. of Washington,
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	In the current investigation, unbiased profiling of the metabolome and lipidome
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	adipose tissue samples (visceral(VAT) and subcutaenous (SAT)) and serum of 50
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	patients, including stages I-IV, from the Fred Hutchinson Cancer Center
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	and the German Cancer Research Center (Heidelberg, Germany) was conducted. The
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	and metabolome of adipose tissue (VAT/SAT) and serum were analyzed using
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	UPLC-QTOFMS analysis and GC-TOFMS analyses, respectively. The primary
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	of this project were to 1) compare the metabolome and lipidome of matched VAT
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	SAT adipose tissue of n=50 Colorectal Cancer Cell (CRC) patients, 2)
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	the associations between the lipidome and metabolome in adipose tissue
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	and serum of n=50 CRC patients and 3) test the associations between the
PR:PROJECT_SUMMARY               	of VAT and serum with the tumor stage of CRC patients.
