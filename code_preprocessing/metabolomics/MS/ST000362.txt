ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	false discovery rate, and metaanalysis were used to test associations between
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	COPD subphenotypes and sphingolipids. Peripheral blood mononuclear cells were
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	used to test associations between sphingolipid gene expression and plasma
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	sphingolipids.

SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	Sphingomyelins, dihydrosphingomyelins, ceramides and dihydroceramides: A
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	modified Bligh-Dyer extraction method was used in the presence of internal
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	standards (IS). Sample analysis was performed in four batches using a Shimadzu
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	10A HPLC system coupled to a TSQ Quantum Ultra triple quadrupole mass
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	spectrometer operated in SRM mode under ESI(+). A Thermo Betasil C18 column was
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	employed to achieve the optimal sensitivity and separation. Data processing was
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	conducted with Xcalibur software (Thermo) to obtain the peak area ratios of
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	analytes to the corresponding internal standards (Supplemental Table 2). A
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	pooled lipid extract from study samples was used as a quality control (QC)
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	sample, and was injected between every 6 study samples to verify the instrument
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	consistency. The peak area ratios were then normalized to the average of QC peak
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	area ratios in the same batch, so that the results for different batches were
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	brought to the same baseline. Sphingoid bases, ceramide-1-phosphate,
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	monohexosylsphingosine, monohexosylceramides, dihexosylceramides,
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	trihexosylceramides, monohydroxylated monohexosylceramides, monohydroxylated
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	dihexosylceramides, sulfatides, and gangliosides: Plasma protein was
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	precipitated with methanol, followed by supernatant collection, drying, and
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	reconstituting with 1:1 methanol/water in the presence of internal standards.
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	Duplicate sample analysis (1 st analysis and 2 nd analysis) was performed in
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	four batches with an online trapping API-4000 system, (consists of a API-4000
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	mass spectrometer, a Leap PAL autosampler, an Agilent 1100 HPLC, and a Shimadzu
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	HPLC) operated in multiple reaction monitoring (MRM) mode under ESI(+) for all
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	species except sulfatides and E2gangliosides ESI(-). An Agilent Eclipse XRB-C18
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	column was used for sphingoid bases and ceramide-1- phosphate and a Varian
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	Metasil C18 column was used for analysis of monohexosylsphingosine, mono-, di-,
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	tri- hexosylceramides as well as their monohydroxylated ceramides. An Agilent
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	Zobax Eclipse Plus C18 column was used for analysis of sulfatides and
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	gangliosides. A Thermo Betasil C18 trapping column was also used for all
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	analytes. Data processing was conducted with Analyst software (Applied Sciences)
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	to obtain the peak area ratios of analytes to the corresponding internal
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	standards. A pooled lipid extract from study samples was used as a quality
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	control (QC) sample, and was injected between every 10 study samples to verify
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	the instrumental consistency. All duplicated sample runs were averaged. The
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	values for each sphingolipid were peak area ratios that were normalized to
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	internal standard and then normalized to QC standards. Values were relative peak
SP:SAMPLEPREP_SUMMARY            	area ratios and unitless.
