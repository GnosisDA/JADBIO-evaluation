ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	Tumor neurospheres were grown in culture until ~90% confluent. Media was changed
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	to contain 1mM acetate. After 24h, 0h time points were collected and media was
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	changed on all other cells to 1mM 13C-acetate containing media. Cells were then
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	collected at their various time points, 1, 3, 24, 48, or 72 hours. Cells were
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	collected into 15mL tubes, spun down at 100xg for 1min and media aspirated.
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	Pellet was washed (not resuspended) in 150mM ammonium acetate. This was then
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	aspirated off and the cells snap frozen and stored at -80C until all time points
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	complete to ship on dry ice. 0h, 1h, and 3h A, B, and C samples will have gTn by
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	HILIC + TCA by GCMS done on them, while 0h, 1h, and 3h D, E, and F will have
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	FAMES and DG & PC analysis done on them. 24h, 48h, and 72h A, B, and C samples
ST:STUDY_SUMMARY                 	will have FAMES analysis done on them.
